# HR Attrition Analysis Project

## Objective
Analyze employee attrition to identify key factors influencing employee turnover and provide actionable recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Excel (Pivot Tables & Charts)

## Key KPIs
- Attrition Rate
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Salary Band
- Attrition vs OverTime
- Attrition by Age Group

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is around 16%.
- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition.
- Lower salary bands have the highest turnover rates.
- Sales and HR departments experience the highest attrition.
- Employees aged 26â€“35 are more likely to leave.

## Recommendations
- Reduce overtime workload or offer compensation.
- Improve salary structure for lower income bands.
- Introduce clear career development paths.
- Implement targeted retention strategies by department.

## Outcome
This analysis helps HR teams take data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and reduce turnover costs.
